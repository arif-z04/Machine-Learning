My IOT project:

SoilSync: Remote Soil moisture Monitoring using LoRa technology

Home kit: 
- Arduino Nano 
- LoRa 1278 (10uF capacitor for powering up)
- LCD Display 
- LEDs (R, G, Y) and Buzzer 

Field kit
- Arduino Nano 
- LoRa 1278 
- TP4056
- MT3608 
- YL69 Soil Sensor
- Solar (connected with 10K,20K resistor to A3)
- Battery (connected with 10K, 10K resistor to A1) 

Soil Moisture Status: 
- Dry: Red  (Buzzer warning)
- Mid-Dry: Yellow 
- Wet: Green 

Data Format: 
Packet: 
| Timestamp | Soil % | Soil_Raw | Soil_Stat | Solar Stat | SolarVolt | BatteryVolt | Battery% |
Example: 
|45000|92|341|WET|ON|2.04|4.3|90|
